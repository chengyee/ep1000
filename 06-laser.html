Laser Cutting

Laser Cutting is a digital subtractive fabrication technique that consists of cutting or engraving of material by the means of a laser.

It can be used on a number of materials such as plastic, wood, cardboard, etc. The process involves cutting material using a powerful and highly accurate laser that focuses on a small area of the material.

Advantages

  • High level of precision and positional accuracy

  • Material saving

  • High speed of production

  • Sharp and clean edges

  • Very little deformation of the material
